# LogicHoot

**Logic Gates & Circuits | iOS App Support**

---

## Overview

LogicHoot is an iOS app for learning and experimenting with digital logic. It pairs structured lessons with interactive circuit simulations and a free-form playground, so you can go from reading theory to building circuits in seconds.

---

## Features

**Learn**
Lessons at Basic, Intermediate, and Advanced levels, each with theory, truth table, and live simulation.

**Playground**
Drag gates onto a free-form canvas, draw wires, toggle inputs, and watch outputs update instantly.

**AI Hoot**
On-device AI assistant that answers logic gate questions in concise, focused responses. No internet needed.

---

## How to Use

1. **Open the Learn Tab**
   Browse lessons by difficulty level. Tap any card to open the full lesson theory, truth table, and interactive simulation.

2. **Explore the Playground**
   Switch to the Playground tab. Drag gates from the sidebar, connect terminals with wires, and toggle the input switches.

3. **Ask the Hoot**
   Type any logic-gate question in the ask bar at the bottom of the Learn tab. The AI Owl answers on-device no internet needed.

---

## Tips

- Start with AND, OR, and NOT they are the building blocks of all other gates.
- Truth tables inside each lesson card are interactive. Toggle inputs to highlight rows.
- Lesson levels are colour-coded: Blue = Basic, Orange = Intermediate, Purple = Advanced.
- The AI Hoot stays on-topic. Off-topic questions are politely declined.
